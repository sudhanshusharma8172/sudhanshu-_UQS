SECTION 1: ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Greenfield University  was established in 1985 and is located in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across Engineering, Management, Science, Arts, and Law. The campus spans 200 acres and is equipped with modern facilities including a central library, hostels, sports complex, and a career development center.

SECTION 2: ADMISSION INFORMATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Admission Process:   
- Applications open every year on 1st March.
- Last date to submit application form: 30th April.
- Entrance exam date: 15th May.
- Result declaration: 1st June.
- Counselling and seat allotment: 10th to 20th June.
- Classes begin: 1st July.

Eligibility Criteria:
- B.Tech: Minimum 60% marks in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- MBA: Graduation in any discipline with minimum 55% marks.
- B.Sc: Minimum 50% marks in 10+2 with Science subjects.
- M.Tech: B.Tech degree with minimum 60% aggregate.
- BCA: Minimum 50% marks in 10+2 with Mathematics.

Required Documents for Admission:
- 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates
- Graduation certificate (for PG programs)
- Transfer certificate from previous institution
- Migration certificate
- Character certificate
- 6 passport-size photographs
- Aadhar card and PAN card copies
- Income certificate (for scholarship applicants)
- Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC if applicable)

SECTION 3: FEE STRUCTURE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Annual Tuition Fees:
- B.Tech (per year): Rs. 85,000
- MBA (per year): Rs. 75,000
- B.Sc (per year): Rs. 30,000
- M.Tech (per year): Rs. 60,000
- BCA (per year): Rs. 40,000
- B.Com (per year): Rs. 25,000
- BA (per year): Rs. 20,000

Additional Fees (one-time at admission):
- Registration fee: Rs. 2,000
- Library security deposit (refundable): Rs. 1,000
- Examination fee (per semester): Rs. 1,500
- Lab fee (for Science and Engineering): Rs. 5,000 per year
- Student activity fund: Rs. 500 per year

Fee Payment:
- Fees can be paid online via university portal or by demand draft.
- Semester fees must be paid before the 15th of July (odd semester) and 15th of January (even semester).
- Late fee penalty: Rs. 50 per day after due date.
- Fee receipt must be preserved throughout the academic year.

SECTION 4: EXAM DATES AND SCHEDULE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Semester Examination Schedule:
- Mid-semester exam (Odd semester): Last week of September
- End-semester exam (Odd semester): November 20 to December 10
- Mid-semester exam (Even semester): Last week of February
- End-semester exam (Even semester): April 15 to May 5
- Supplementary/Back exams: July (for failed subjects)

Practical Examinations:
- Practical exams are held one week before the theory exams.
- Students must complete 75% attendance in practicals to appear.

Result Declaration:
- Mid-semester results: Within 7 days of the last exam.
- End-semester results: Within 30 days.
- Results are published on the university website and student portal.

Re-evaluation and Rechecking:
- Application for re-evaluation must be submitted within 15 days of result.
- Re-evaluation fee: Rs. 500 per subject.
- Rechecking fee: Rs. 200 per subject.

SECTION 5: SYLLABUS AND CURRICULUM
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Curriculum Structure:
- B.Tech program follows an 8-semester structure (4 years).
- MBA program is of 4 semesters (2 years).
- Each semester has 5 to 6 core subjects plus electives.
- B.Tech students must complete a final year project (FYP) in semester 7 and 8.

Available Electives (B.Tech):
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Cybersecurity
- Cloud Computing
- Data Science
- Blockchain Technology
- Robotics and Automation

Grading System:
- University follows a 10-point CGPA grading system.
- Grade O (Outstanding): 91-100 marks = 10 points
- Grade A+ (Excellent): 81-90 marks = 9 points
- Grade A (Very Good): 71-80 marks = 8 points
- Grade B+ (Good): 61-70 marks = 7 points
- Grade B (Above Average): 51-60 marks = 6 points
- Grade C (Average): 40-50 marks = 5 points
- Grade F (Fail): Below 40 marks = 0 points
- Minimum CGPA required for degree: 5.0

Syllabus download is available on the university official website under the Academics section.

SECTION 6: LIBRARY INFORMATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Library Timings:
- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Sunday: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- Library remains closed on national holidays and during semester break.

Library Rules:
- Students must carry their university ID card to enter the library.
- Mobile phones must be kept on silent mode inside the library.
- Food and beverages are strictly not allowed inside.
- Maximum 3 books can be issued at one time to a student.
- Book loan period: 14 days. Can be renewed once for another 7 days.
- Fine for late return: Rs. 2 per day per book.
- Lost or damaged book fine: Replacement cost + Rs. 200 penalty.
- Reference books, journals, and newspapers cannot be issued, only read inside.
- Group study rooms are available on the second floor; must be booked one day in advance.

Digital Library:
- Students have access to NPTEL, JSTOR, IEEE Xplore, and Shodhganga.
- Login credentials are provided at the start of each academic year.
- Remote access to digital library is available through the student portal.

SECTION 7: HOSTEL INFORMATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Hostel Facilities:
- Separate hostels for boys and girls.
- Boys Hostel: Blocks A, B, and C (total 600 rooms).
- Girls Hostel: Blocks D and E (total 400 rooms).
- Each room accommodates 2 students (double sharing).
- Facilities: WiFi, attached bathroom, study table, wardrobe, ceiling fan.

Hostel Fee:
- Hostel fee (per year): Rs. 45,000 (includes room + meals)
- Mess charges (per month): Rs. 3,000
- Hostel security deposit (refundable): Rs. 5,000

Hostel Rules:
- Hostel entry timings: Students must return by 10:00 PM on weekdays and 11:00 PM on weekends.
- Outsiders and friends are NOT allowed inside hostel rooms.
- Visitors can only meet students in the visitor's lounge between 4 PM and 7 PM.
- Consumption of alcohol, drugs, and tobacco is strictly prohibited and leads to immediate expulsion.
- Playing loud music after 10 PM is not permitted.
- Students must keep their rooms clean and tidy at all times.
- Any damage to hostel property will be charged to the responsible student.
- Students must inform the warden before going home or outstation.
- Night stay outside campus requires prior written permission from the warden.
- Ragging of any kind is strictly prohibited and is a punishable offense.
- Any electrical appliances (heaters, irons) require prior permission from warden.

SECTION 8: PLACEMENT AND CAREER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Placement Statistics (2023-2024):
- Total students placed: 85% of eligible students
- Highest package: Rs. 24 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum)
- Average package: Rs. 6.5 LPA
- Total companies visited: 120+
- On-campus placement drives start from: October (for final year students)

Top Recruiters:
- TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL Technologies, Tech Mahindra
- Amazon, Flipkart, Zomato, Swiggy
- HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank
- Deloitte, Accenture, Cognizant
- L&T, Tata Motors, Mahindra

Placement Eligibility:
- Minimum CGPA of 6.0 required to sit for campus placements.
- No active backlogs (failed subjects) at the time of placement.
- Students must register on the placement portal before September 30.

Training and Preparation:
- Pre-placement training starts from 3rd year, covering aptitude, reasoning, and communication.
- Mock interviews and group discussion sessions are conducted regularly.
- Industry visits and guest lectures are organized each semester.

Career Development Center (CDC):
- CDC helps students with resume building, interview preparation, and career counselling.
- Located in the Administrative Block, Room 105.
- Timing: Monday to Friday, 10 AM to 5 PM.

SECTION 9: SCHOLARSHIPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
University Scholarships:
1. Merit Scholarship:
   - For students scoring 90% or above in 10+2.
   - 25% waiver on annual tuition fee.
   - Must maintain CGPA of 8.5 or above each year to continue.

2. Sports Scholarship:
   - For students who have represented state or national level sports.
   - 20% waiver on tuition fee.
   - Proof of participation required.

3. Need-Based Scholarship:
   - For students whose family income is below Rs. 2.5 lakh per year.
   - Up to 50% fee waiver.
   - Requires income certificate and bank statement.

4. SC/ST Scholarship:
   - Fully funded by Government of Uttar Pradesh.
   - Students must apply through UP Scholarship Portal (scholarship.up.gov.in).
   - Caste certificate and income certificate required.

5. OBC Scholarship:
   - Partially funded by state government.
   - Apply through UP Scholarship Portal.

Government Scholarships:
- National Scholarship Portal (NSP): nationalscho­larship.gov.in
- Students can check eligibility and apply online.
- Last date to apply: Usually 30th November each year.

Scholarship Application Process:
- Fill the scholarship form available at the Accounts Department or online portal.
- Attach all required documents.
- Submit before the deadline (31st October for university scholarships).
- Results are declared within 60 days.
